Welcome to dreaM House!

dreaM House is an end to end Property Management Company providing best services. We are there to solve all the problem related to property. dreaM House has been founded with the purpose of providing trustworthy property management services in India and providing best properties. In a country like India, where resources are scarce, Non-Resident owners are often compelled to lock their houses due to a lack of property management options. dreaM House believes that a large resource like an empty house should be put to optimal use. This is good for the customer. As India’s leading property management company, dreaM House is working to unlock those locked doors. Our team comprises highly educated and experienced professionals. We believe in service and commitment. You trust us with your property and we take that seriously. Our team will overcome obstacles, find solutions, and deliver exceptional results. This is what makes us one of the best property management companies in India.

Why Choose Us

  • dreaM House is a new home rental platform that makes it easier to find your new home without paying any brokerage. Use our verified listing to ensure you find your dream home by contacting the owners directly. In case you cannot find a property you are looking for, please post a requirement and we'll send you email notification with properties matching your requirements.

  • dreaM House is a disruptive real-estate platform that makes it possible to buy/sell/rent a house without paying any brokerage

  • dreaM House was started because all of us believed that paying hefty brokerage can not be the only option to find a new home. As tenants, we have been paying these brokerages year on year without seeing any advantage of the broker. The only reason he existed was that there was a huge information asymmetry in the market. dreaM House is a platform that removes this information asymmetry and provides a marketplace for free exchange of this information that used to cost 1-2 months of rent as brokerage.

  • We have done 2 things to help you find that perfect home:

    • Firstly, we have painstakingly verified each listing and made sure that these are direct owners or shared accommodation parties and there are no middlemen or brokers. We use lot of heuristics and techniques to ensure that you get a totally broker free list.

    • Secondly, we have also tried to ensure that maximum information is available to you in as easy to use format. This ensures that you get a very good idea of the property even before you visit it. Thus, you can shortlist flats sitting at the comfort of your home without actually traveling all the good and bad properties. This saves your time and effort and with a quick shortlist of few properties you can actually get a house in few hours! If you are a landlord interested in posting your apartments to dreaM House, please email us at hello@dreaM House.in and we will get in touch to help you list the property.
